Screening Task: Power Systems Simulation using OpenModelica and OpenIPSL (For Power Systems Engineering background students)

  1. By using the OpenIPSL library in OpenModelica, Create one to three models. You can submit any number of models maximum being three and the screening would be done based on the complexity of the models.
  2. Take any power system network (from a project report, websites, textbook or a journal) and model it in OpenModelica using OpenIPSL library
  3. Please make sure that the submitted simulations are not under the Completed Power System Simulations.

Technical Requirements: Knowledge of OpenModelica & OpenIPSL.

Procedure to Submit:

Step1: Procedure to propose simulations

  1. Select any power system networks (from a project report, textbook, websites or a journal) which you are going to model.
  2. Propose a model you wish to simulate using proposal form here.
  3. You can propose up to three simulations using the above form and if you would like to submit more than three models, Kindly mail us at contact[hyphen]pssp[at]fossee[dot]in.
  4. Before submitting a proposal, make sure you have a proper description and an abstract ready about the same.
  5. Kindly upload the abstract in PDF format. Refer to the sample abstract provided here.
  6. Please note that if you are proposing two models you must have two different abstracts
  7. Please make sure that the submitted simulations are not under the Completed Power system simulations.
  8. Your proposal will be reviewed and approved/disapproved (within one working day).
  9. After receiving the approval of your proposal through email, you can proceed to step 2.

Step 2: Procedure to submit simulation

  1. After getting your approval complete the simulation of the models.
  2. Once the simulations are ready you can submit them by clicking here. 

Evaluation Criteria: Following criteria will be considered while evaluating the model:

  1. The Complexity of the model
  2. Accuracy of results obtained
